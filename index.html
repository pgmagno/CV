My Skills.

Teacher

Then I became a teacher at an English course and so have I been for 10 years now, and I grew quite fond of it, when the conditions are satisfactory that is.

Programmer

Programming, on the other hand, was actually my first choice, but being the green unexperienced boy that I was, and lacking the family support necessary to see it through, I left a bachelor course in Informational Systems after two semesters.

More than ten years later, and always developing myself indirectly, I finally got back to it. In 2020, I mustered up the courage to believe in myself and so I started a course of Software Engineering which now really feels like the best decision ever.
